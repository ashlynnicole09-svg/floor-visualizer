Help

  1. Upload your room photo.
  2. Wait for the photo to appear on the stage (status will show “Photo loaded”).
  3. Click 4 floor corners in order (clockwise).
  4. Select or upload a flooring texture.
  5. Use Measure Tool: draw a line over a known length (e.g., 36″ doorway), enter the inches, and click “Set Scale”.
  6. Adjust plank size, angle, and opacity.
  7. Export PNG to share.
